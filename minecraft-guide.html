▶ SECTION 01

TIPS TO BEAT
MINECRAFT

🏆
ACHIEVEMENT UNLOCKED
KNOW HOW TO WIN
01

Find a Village on Day 1. Villages give you beds, food, tools and iron from blacksmiths — they can save hours of early grinding. Sleep every night to skip Phantoms.

02

Rush Iron, not Wood. Stone tools break fast. Prioritize finding 9+ iron ore in the first 10 minutes. An iron sword + pickaxe changes the early game entirely.

03

Enchant with Efficiency IV + Fortune III. Before mining diamonds, craft a bookshelf enchanting room. Fortune III triples your diamond yield — a massive multiplier.

04

Nether travel = superhighway. 1 block in the Nether = 8 blocks in the Overworld. Build a Nether road to get to strongholds and biomes in seconds.

05

Find the Stronghold FAST. Throw an Eye of Ender near your spawn to get directional guidance. Most strongholds are 1,000–3,000 blocks from spawn.

06

Ender Dragon Strategy: Destroy all End Crystals on the pillars first (shoot with bow). Then perch on a pillar and spam arrows at the dragon when it hovers the fountain.

07

Bring beds to the End fight. Beds explode in the End dimension — place and sleep next to the dragon while it charges the fountain for massive, cheap damage.

08

Golden Apples + Potions for the fight. Bring Strength II, Regeneration, Invisibility potions. Pack at least 4 Golden Apples for healing during the dragon fight.

09

Strip-mine at Y = -58 for Diamonds. In 1.18+, diamonds are most common between Y -58 and -64. Strip mining at this level with Fortune III is the most efficient method.

10

Shield = game changer. Craft a shield as early as possible. It negates 100% of frontal melee damage and blocks arrow damage completely. Always have one equipped.

11

Trade with villagers for enchanted books. Librarian villagers sell Mending, Silk Touch and Protection IV books. Lock trades by curing zombie villagers for huge discounts.

12

Never dig straight down. Classic rule — always mine in a 2-block staircase pattern to avoid falling into lava or voids below your feet.

BEST FARMS TO BUILD

🌾
AFK IRON GOLEM FARM

Build in a village with 20+ beds and 20+ villagers. Golems spawn automatically, fall into a lava blade and drop Iron Ingots. Produces 300–800 ingots/hour while AFK.

Redstone 800/hr
⭐
XP BLAZE FARM

Build a platform around a Nether Fortress blaze spawner. Funnel blazes into a kill chamber you can one-shot. Fastest XP in the game — max-enchant a tool in 5 minutes.

Fast XP Nether
🐠
GUARDIAN FARM

Drain an ocean monument and convert it into a guardian farm. Produces Prismarine, Sea Lanterns and massive XP. Takes time to build but produces ~180k items per hour.

Prismarine Complex
🥩
COW/CHICKEN COOKER

Breed cows or chickens above a hopper-connected lava blade. Babies grow up, get cooked instantly and drop cooked meat into chests. Infinite food supply forever.

Cooked Meat Passive
🕸️
SPIDER / STRING FARM

Build on top of a spider spawner in a cave. A water funnel pushes spiders to a kill zone. You get endless String (for bows, wool) and Spider Eyes (for potions).

String Spider Eye
💎
VILLAGER TRADING HALL

Cure zombie villagers to get maximum discount. Set up rows of Librarians, Farmers, Weaponsmiths and Fletchers. Trade paper/carrots for emeralds; emeralds for enchanted gear. Unlimited diamonds.

Emeralds Best Farm
🎆
ENDERMAN FARM

Build a large platform in the End at Y=128. Endermen fall from the height and you one-shot them. Insane Ender Pearl drops and XP — best late-game XP farm in the game.

End Only Top XP
🌵
CACTUS + BAMBOO FARM

A zero-tick cactus farm using pistons auto-harvests cactus into hoppers. Bamboo auto-breaks itself. Combine both for infinite fuel — cactus for green dye, bamboo for scaffolding and sticks.

Fuel Zero-Tick
🍄
MUSHROOM / BONE MEAL FARM

Use a Composter with a skeleton farm to generate infinite Bone Meal. Feed it into a flower/crop farm for auto-growing food. Essential for early game food security on any difficulty.

Bone Meal Crops
